About Our System

Water turbidity refers to the degree to which water is cloudy or murky due to the presence of suspended particles or impurities. Managing water turbidity is important for several reasons, including ensuring that water is safe for consumption, protecting aquatic life, and maintaining water quality.

Managing water turbidity is important for a number of reasons, including:

  • Health: Turbid water can contain harmful bacteria, viruses, and parasites that can cause waterborne diseases.
  • Aesthetics: Turbid water can be unappealing and uninviting to drink, which can discourage people from using it.
  • Industrial and agricultural applications: Turbid water can interfere with industrial and agricultural processes that require clean water.
